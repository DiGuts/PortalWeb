# Product

## Register

product

## Users

500+ employees across every level of a Catalan industrial manufacturer. Engineers and technicians on desktop, mechanics and electricians checking from phones on the factory floor, peons on brief breaks, admin and comercials at desks, managers approving requests. Mix of desk-native and mobile-first users. Each role has a different primary workflow and zero patience for UI friction.

## Product Purpose

Internal employee portal for Tavil Industrial S.A.U. (packaging and end-of-line machinery manufacturer, Sant Jaume de Llierca). Features: company news, activities, agenda, courses, vacation requests, suggestions, incident reports, surveys, notifications, admin backoffice, quiz.

Success: employees check it every morning before their shift. Managers approve vacation requests in under 30 seconds. The CEO can open it in front of a client and not flinch. Every role finds its workflow immediately.

## Brand Personality

Bold, modern, intuitive, trustworthy, polished, corporate. The accent is Mahogany Red — present and confident, never decorative. Human-friendly warmth over cold enterprise authority. Feels like it was made with care, not generated.

## Anti-references

- Generic enterprise grey (SAP, Workday, old-school HR portals)
- SaaS-blue-and-white commodity design (Jira, default MUI, cookie-cutter dashboards)
- AI slop aesthetics: gradient text, hero-metric cards, identical icon-heading-text grids, glassmorphism for its own sake
- "Modern design basics" — the homogeneous look of every vibe-coded web app in 2024: Inter font, soft shadows, muted palette, rounded everything, no character

## Design Principles

1. **Red with restraint.** Mahogany Red is the identity anchor of the brand. It earns every pixel it occupies. It signals action, importance, and ownership — not decoration.
2. **Role-fluidity.** An engineer and a peon arrive at the same portal and each finds their path in seconds. No role is an afterthought.
3. **Human over corporate.** Warm, readable, and approachable. The factory floor user checking vacation status on a phone in 60 seconds matters as much as the admin managing content on a desktop.
4. **Polished without performance.** Looks boardroom-ready but is genuinely fast and usable. No shimmer, no decoration that doesn't earn its place.
5. **Earned confidence.** Every interaction is deliberate and solid. Nothing feels approximate, generated, or assembled from a template.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No hard WCAG level mandated. Target WCAG AA as working baseline: large, age-diverse workforce, factory floor mobile use, varied screen sizes and lighting conditions. Reduced motion always respected.
